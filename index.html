About

Ini-Ememobong

Ini Ememobong is a Nigerian politician. He is the current commissioner for information and strategy in Akwa Ibom State and the immediate past state publicity secretary of Peoples Democratic Party in Akwa Ibom State of Nigeria. He is the founder of Ini Ememobong Foundation.

He was born on 7 November 1983. He is the second son in the family of Mkpisong Ememobong and Rosemary Essien.[2] He started his education at Calabar Preparatory Nursery School. In 1987, at the creation of Akwa Ibom State, his family relocated to Ikot Ekpene where he continued his primary education at Holy Child International Nursery and Primary School, Ikot Ekpene.............
